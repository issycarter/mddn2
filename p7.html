What is the Voyager?

Since the 1977 launch, space craft Voyager 1 has been progressing through the solar system towards the heliosphere and interstellar space. Voyager 1 was launched to explore the solar system, and to measure high-energy particles called galactic cosmic rays. In 2012, Voyager 1 reached an area of the heliosphere especially high in radiation and cosmic rays, suggesting it had approached interstellar space.

Deep space communication is very important to reach spacecrafts such as Voyager 1 as it travels and makes new discoveries of the outer solar system.

Over the next ten years, Voyager 1 is expected to keep in contact with the DSN on earth, until approximately 2025 where it will travel too far out, or run out of processing power.
